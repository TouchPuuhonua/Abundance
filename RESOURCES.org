** Moral Psychology

[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moral_psychology][Moral Psychology]] is the study of human thought and behavior in ethical

beings.

communicate across cultural differences!  Without this understanding learners will all-to-easily get trapped in parochial viewpoints.

** More Communication Resources

*** NVC: Non-Violent Communication

Marshall Rosenberg and his associates developed an extraordinary model
of communication based on deep empathic listening.  I like to call it
"Empathic Communication" and it's usually just called NVC.

Many books, videos and workshops teach the NVC model and how to use it
effectively.

**** An NVC Study (Reading & Practice) Group

One of the best ways to learn NVC is to form a reading group with a
few friends or neighbors who are interested in communication.  An NVC
reading group generally reads the two books:
- /Nonviolent Communication: A Language of Life/ by Marshall Rosenberg
- /Nonviolent Communication Companion Workbook, 2nd Edition: A
  Practical Guide for Individual, Group, or Classroom Study/ by Lucy
  Leu
a chapter every week.  Lucy's book has chapters corresponding to
Marshall's chapters so your study group can read one chapter from each
book every week or 2 weeks (or however often you meet).  Everyone does
their best to practice what they read in their daily lives and then
comes together to discuss what they discovered.

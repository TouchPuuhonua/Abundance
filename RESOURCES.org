** Moral Psychology

*** [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jonathan_Haidt][Jonathan Haidt]] :wikipedia:moral_psychologist:know_thyself:

[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moral_psychology][Moral Psychology]] is the study of human thought and behavior in ethical
contexts.  It is one of the keys to understanding who we are as human
beings.

Jonathan Haidt's particular work shows how to understand people and
communicate across cultural differences!  Without this understanding learners will all-to-easily get trapped in parochial viewpoints.

- [[https://www.ted.com/talks/jonathan_haidt_the_moral_roots_of_liberals_and_conservatives][The moral roots of liberals and conservatives]] :video:tedtalk:
- [[http://righteousmind.com][The Righteous Mind]] :book:
- [[https://jonathanhaidt.com][Jonathan Haidt]] :personal_website:

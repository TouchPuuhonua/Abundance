Activating Partnership Culture: An Interactive Workshop

Presenter: Touch Pu'uhonua
Time: 90 minutes

* What It's All About

This workshop unifies key New Culture elements through the lens of
Riane Eisler's Partnership Culture into a synthesis which is
- more comprehensible and communicable
- better for liberating ourselves and others
- a basis for connecting with New Culture Allies

As the synthesis progresses
- participants will be working together in small groups
- untangling confusion in real-life scenarios

The group practice will yield a practical boost of
- clarity with our glorious New Culture Experiment
- effective communication and shareable power

* The Workshop Structure

We'll acknowledge The Glory Of New Culture
- Consent, NVC, HAI, Active Communication, Body Acceptance, ...
- So much liberation!
- Ecstatic personal transformation!
- Nurturing connections!

We'll address the elephants in the room:
- It's hard to share radical ideas with others!
- We're so far out there it can be alienating!
- And shouldn't liberation provide more practical power?

We'll bring in Riane Eisler's *Partnership Culture*
- mixed with an understanding of
  - *Fundamental Human Interactions* (Win-Win, Win-Lose, ...)
  - *Abundance vs. Scarcity* Consciousness
  - with a dash of Jonathan Haidt & Abraham Maslow

The presenter will interact with the whole workshop
- then the small groups will put it to use with a scenario
- then the groups will share their results to the whole room
and so on for 3-4 rounds as time allows

We'll end with final questions, shares and hurrahs!

* The Context of Abundance / Partnership / Win-Win / etc.

** What do we mean by Abundance, etc. here?

Human Beings are social creatures who
- Grew Up In and Inhabit one or more Societies

All Societies have a Culture
- a Giant Shared Context (aka a Paradigm)
which shapes the lives of everyone in the Society
- often (usually!) without them recognizing that shaping!

We're distinguishing Abundance as a Social Context
- a Context for how you live your life
- and especially as you interact with others!

We don't mean Abundance in the sense of
- Having plenty of whatever you want!

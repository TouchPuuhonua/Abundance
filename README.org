* The Context of Abundance / Partnership / Win-Win / etc.

** What do we mean by Abundance, etc. here?

We're distinguishing Abundance as a Context

We don't mean Abundance in the sense of
- Having plenty of whatever you want!

We mean Abundance as
- a Context for how you live your life
- and especially as you interact with others!
